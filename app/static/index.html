Out of scope (deliberate demo boundaries)
  • Full session information and semantic IDs are not considered (e.g. SASRec-style sequential recommenders).
  • Full RecSys evaluation (NDCG, MRR) is not considered — it requires crafting ground-truth relevance data.
  • Product catalogue data size is limited to what is meaningful within synthetic generation.
  • Observability and logging beyond basic request logs are not considered due to the missing evaluation loop.
  • Online performance measurement on test/control groups, A/A tests and causal inference is not considered.
  • User action feedback (clicks, views, impressions) is not considered — there is no full shopping portal or customer-journey data.
